About My Hometown

Sidoarjo is a district in East Java Province, Indonesia. The capital is Sidoarjo District. This district borders directly on Surabaya City and Gresik Regency to the north, Madura Strait to the east, Pasuruan Regency to the south, and Mojokerto Regency to the west. Together with Gresik, Sidoarjo is one of the main supports of the city of Surabaya, and is included in the Pintukertosusila area. The population of this district is 2,033,764 people in 2021. This district is the former kingdom of Jenggala (the era of the Kediri-Majapahit kingdom) and also the former Duchy of Terung (the Subordinate Duchy of Majapahit during the late Majapahit era).
