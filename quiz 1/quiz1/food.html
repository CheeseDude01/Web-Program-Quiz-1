Local Food

About The Local Food

Sidoarjo has a variety of local foods. But I currently have two favorites, that is Ote-Ote Porong and Kupang Lontong.

Ote-Ote Porong is fried fritter not to dissimilar to normal Ote-Ote, but instead of shredded cabbage, carrots, and shrimp as the filling, Ote-Ote Porong is filled with chicken, seaweed, and oysters. It is absolutely delicious and dangerously addictive.

Kupang Lontong is a dish that consist of Lontong topped with Kupang (a species of small mussels) and covered in a seafood broth where the Kupang was cooked. It is sweet, savory, and healthy. The kupang provides a sweet seafoody taste, while the broth is savory and heartwarming. It is definitely a must try when you visit Sidoarjo.
